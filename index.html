Operation Sindoor

Operation Sindoor is an initiative aimed at empowering rural women through education and skill development. Launched in 2020, it focuses on creating sustainable livelihood opportunities and raising awareness on health and social issues.

Key Objectives

  • Provide vocational training in tailoring, handicrafts, and agriculture.
  • Promote digital literacy among women in rural areas.
  • Increase awareness about women's health and legal rights.

Community Impact

Since its inception, Operation Sindoor has reached over 10,000 women across multiple states, improving their economic conditions and boosting self-confidence.
